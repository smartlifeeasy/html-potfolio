Education

Alabi Tolwani's educational background includes foundational schooling in Nigeria and specialized medical training abroad. He completed his primary education at Yemi International Primary School in Ogun State, Nigeria. For high school, he attended Federal Government College in Ikole-Ekiti, Nigeria, where he spent three years each in junior and senior school. Following his secondary education, he pursued higher studies at Shenyang Medical College, focusing on medical training and education. This solid educational foundation supports his career as a medical practitioner, entrepreneur, and digital marketer.
